SSC-100 (Pre-Neutralised Carbomers)

Appearance

Hygroscopic white powder

Chemical Compound

Carboxy vinyl polymer sodium salt Sodium Carbomer

Commercial Name : Pre-Neutralized Carbomer
(Sodium Carbomer)

INCI Name : Sodium Carbomer

CAS Number : 9003-04

Uses

Rheology modifier for clear gels, hydro-alcoholic gels and creams.

Provides high viscosity and forms clear gels.

Short flow and non-drip properties.

Benefits of SSC-100
(Pre-Neutralised Carbomer)

Superior handling (low dusting powder)

Quick dispersion

Simple production process (no need for neutralization step)

Consistent pH throughout production

Allows for easy viscosity adjustment in finished products
