FAQ’s

SSC-100

( Pre-Neutralised Carbomer )

What is Pre-Neutralized Carbomer?

Pre-Neutralized carbomer is a type of carbomer polymer that has been pre-neutralized with an alkaline substance, typically sodium hydroxide or triethanolamine. This preneutralization simplifies the formulation process by eliminating the need for additional neutralization steps during product preparation.

What are the common uses of Pre-Neutralized Carbomer?

Pre-Neutralized carbomers are widely used in the cosmetics and personal care industry as thickening agents, stabilizers, and viscosity modifiers in formulations such as creams, lotions, gels,sanitizer and topical products.

How does Pre-Neutralized Carbomer differ from traditional carbomer?

Traditional carbomer polymers are supplied in a powdered form and require neutralization with an alkaline substance, such as sodium hydroxide or triethanolamine, to achieve their thickening and gelling properties. Preneutralized carbomers, on the other hand, are already neutralized, making them easier to handle and incorporate into formulations.

What are the benefits of using Pre-Neutralized Carbomer?

Some benefits of Pre-Neutralized carbomer include:
- Simplified formulation process: Eliminates the need for neutralization, reducing formulation time and complexity.
- Consistency and reliability: Pre-Neutralized carbomers offer consistent performance and viscosity control, resulting in more predictable product outcomes.
- Reduced risk of pH variability: Since the neutralization step is already completed, there is less chance of pH fluctuations in the final product.
.

Is Pre-Neutralized Carbomer suitable for all types of formulations?

Pre-Neutralized carbomer is commonly used in a wide range of cosmetic and personal care formulations. However, its suitability may depend on factors such as the specific requirements of the formulation, desired viscosity, compatibility with other ingredients, and regulatory considerations.

Are there any limitations or considerations when using Pre-Neutralized Carbomer?

While Pre-Neutralized carbomers offer several advantages, it's essential to consider factors such as compatibility with other ingredients, potential sensitivity or irritation issues, and stability under varying storage conditions. Additionally, manufacturers should follow recommended usage levels and formulation guidelines provided by the supplier.

How should Pre-Neutralized Carbomer be stored?

Pre-Neutralized carbomer should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture to maintain its stability and performance. It is essential to follow the storage recommendations provided by the manufacturer to prevent degradation or changes in quality over time.

Is Pre-Neutralized Carbomer compatible with other common cosmetic ingredients?

Pre-Neutralized carbomer is generally compatible with a wide range of cosmetic ingredients, including emollients, humectants, preservatives, and active compounds. However, compatibility testing should be conducted when formulating new products to ensure optimal performance and stability.
